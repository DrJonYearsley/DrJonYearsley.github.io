International Day of Biological Diversity 2021: Outreach Videos

For International Day of Biological Diversity 2021 we asked colleagues to answer questions from children at the Monkstown Educate Together National School.

These are their great questions:

  • Why does grass grow?
  • Why do dears have antlers?
  • Why are sticks brown?
  • How long can trees live?
  • How does moss grow?
  • Why is grass green?
  • Why is soil biodiversity important?
  • What is water made of?
  • What is wood made of?
  • What is biodiversity?
  • Why do humans have tailbones?

This initiative is part of our EPA funded phenology project (PHENOGRASS).
